Ikenfell

a turnbased-rpg with timing mechanics

Ikenfell is a turnbased-rpg that came out on october 8th 2020. The protagonist is Maritte Hildegaard, a girl without any magical talent.
She travels to Ikenfell, a school of magic, in search of her sister when she doesn't come home one summer.
When she gets there, she finds there is something very strange going on...
